Bruno Yamazaky

Bruno Yamazaky is a Portuguese amateur runner who began his athletic career as a federated table tennis player with the FPTM. He also dabbled in track and field during his youth. After a decade-long break, Bruno has returned to sports, focusing on long-distance running and participating in various road and trail events.
