Bruno Peixoto

Bruno Peixoto is a Portuguese amateur runner who began his athletic career as a federated table tennis player with the FPTM. He also dabbled in track and field during his youth. After a decade-long break, Bruno has returned to sports, focusing on long-distance running and participating in various road and trail events. He also occasionally competes under the name Bruno Eira, particularly when representing NAVR.

He is the cousin of Tiago Eira da Silva, another runner featured on this website.
